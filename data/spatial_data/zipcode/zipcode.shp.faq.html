  2. How were the data generated, processed, and modified?

    Date: 25-Jul-2003 (process 1 of 10)
    Zipcode has been updated with January 2003 Postal Service Five Digit Zip Code maps and Washington State Department of Revenue Zip Code database.

    Date: 31-Jul-2008 (process 2 of 10)
    Overlay E-site data (point data combined with E911 and USPS addresses) on top of zipcode and parcel data layers. Modify zipcode polygon, divide into smaller polygon with carrier route ID and aline with parcel data.

    Data sources used in this process:

    • E911 (May 2007)

    Date: 31-Jul-2008 (process 3 of 10)
    Dissolve by Zip, Zip Type, County.

    Date: 20-Sep-2010 (process 4 of 10)
    Update Zip Codes for East King County using E-911 address point data. Zipcodes include 98010, 11, 14, 19, 22, 24, 25, 27, 29, 34, 38, 41, 45, 50, 51, 53, 65, 72, 74, 75, 77, 92

    Date: 12-Nov-2010 (process 5 of 10)
    Update Zip Codes for much of suburban King County using E-911 address point data. Zipcodes include 98001, 02, 03, 06, 28, 30, 31, 33, 39, 42, 47, 52, 55, 56, 57, 58, 59, 98146, 48, 58, 68, 78, 98354. Minor changes to 98003, 04, 07, 08, 11, 32, 40, 75, 77, 92, 98106, 08, 18, 88. Removed 98054 (obsolete).

    Date: 26-Jul-2011 (process 6 of 10)
    Updated 98011,98028,98074 and 98072, ZIP boundary change for Kirkland North Juanita Annexation. Updated 98031, 98055 and 98058, boundary change for Kent Panther Lake Annexation. Updated 98032 and 98198, western boundary of Kent along Pacific Hwy S.

    Date: 28-Jul-2011 (process 7 of 10)
    Metadata imported.
